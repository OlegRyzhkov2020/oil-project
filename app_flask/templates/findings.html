Key Findings and Observations
MARKET FUNDUMENTAL FACTORS

1) Oil's use in fuels continues to be the primary factor in making it a high-demand commodity around the globe

2) Rising Oil Rig Count and Increase in Oil Production Puts Prices Under Pressure

NON MARKET FORCES

1) Plate tectonics is also responsible for creating the "pressure cooker" that slowly matures the organics into oil and gas. 2) The world experiences perpetuating patterns of military conflict, followed by oil supply crises and changes in the oil countries market positions

FORECAST AND PREDICTION MODEL

1) The ARIMA model does a relatively good job of predicting the actual BRENT and WTI test data from 2016 to 2020 2) There is a statistically significant linear relatiionship of Oil Production, Oil Rig Count and Fuel Consumption with Oil Prices

Future Research
